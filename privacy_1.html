Privacy Notice

How we handle the information you share with us, and the rights you have over it.

Last updated: 2026.

MEHR Partners ("MEHR", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This notice explains what personal information we collect when you contact us or use this website, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have. It is written to align with common data-protection standards, including the EU/UK GDPR.

Who we are

MEHR Partners is an operator-led partnership. For any question about this notice or your personal information, contact us at info@mehrpartners.com.

What we collect

We only collect what you choose to give us. When you complete our contact form or email us, that typically includes:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your company or organisation, if you provide it
  • The content of your message and any details you share about your enquiry
  • Basic technical data your browser sends, and limited anti-spam signals used to protect the form

Why we use it, and our lawful basis

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiry, to have the resulting conversation, and to provide any services you go on to engage us for. Where the GDPR applies, our lawful basis is your consent (given when you submit the form) and our legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries. We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling, and we do not sell it.

Who we share it with

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing. We may share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate, for example our form and email providers, and only so they can perform that service for us. We may also disclose information where we are legally required to do so.

International transfers

We operate across the United States, Europe, the Gulf, and Asia, and our service providers may process data outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry information for as long as needed to handle your request and to maintain a reasonable record of our correspondence, then delete or anonymise it. If we begin a formal engagement, related records are retained under that engagement's agreement and applicable law.

How we protect it

We apply reasonable organisational and technical measures to protect your information, keep access limited to the partners, and use reputable providers for our website and communications.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, to correct or delete it, to restrict or object to its use, to withdraw consent, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email us at info@mehrpartners.com and we will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

Cookies

This site uses only what is necessary to function and to protect our contact form from abuse. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Changes

We may update this notice from time to time. The current version is always the one published here.
